## 1. Scope of services <a href="#hlk74145951" id="hlk74145951"></a>

### **1.1. Scope of services.**

The Component Manager gives the Client better control over her Adobe Analytics components in a GoogleSheet. "Components" refer to Segments, Metrics ("Success Events" and built-in Metrics like "Visits"), Calculated Metrics, Dimensions (eVars, props, Classifications and built-in dimensions like "Site Section"), Date Ranges, Workspaces and Virtual Report Suites.\
